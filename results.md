# Results

## Example Rebuttal Table

The table below demonstrates a completed rebuttal for a hypothetical manuscript.

| # | Reviewer Comment | Author Response | Manuscript Change |
|---|-----------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | The introduction lacks sufficient background on prior work. | We agree and have expanded the introduction to include a review of key prior studies. | Added two paragraphs and five new references in the Introduction (p. 2, lines 10–30). |
| 2 | The sample size seems small; please justify your choice. | The sample size was determined by a power analysis (α = 0.05, power = 0.80). We have added this justification to the Methods section. | Added power analysis details in the Methods section (p. 5, lines 4–8). |
| 3 | Figure 3 is unclear and hard to read. | We have redrawn Figure 3 with higher resolution and improved labelling. | Figure 3 replaced with a revised, higher-resolution version (p. 9). |
| 4 | The discussion does not address potential limitations. | A limitations paragraph has been added to the Discussion section. | New paragraph added at the end of the Discussion (p. 12, lines 45–55). |
